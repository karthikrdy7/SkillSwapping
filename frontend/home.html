Learning About Skill Swapping

ü§ù What is Skill Swapping?

Skill swapping is a collaborative learning approach where people exchange knowledge and expertise. Instead of traditional one-way teaching, both participants teach and learn from each other, creating a mutually beneficial learning experience.

üí° How Does It Work?

Our platform matches you with people based on complementary skills. If you know programming and want to learn design, we'll connect you with designers who want to learn programming. You can then schedule sessions, share resources, and learn from each other at your own pace.
